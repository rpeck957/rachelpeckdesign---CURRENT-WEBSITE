Canisius College 2016 Quadrangle Literary Magazine

2016

Annual literary magazine design. Design includes all visual elements such as layout, content placement, typography, etc.

I was chosen to be the co-designer of the 2016 issue of the Quadrangle. We both developed the design & all visual elements of the magazine & I was mainly responsible for the layout. This was published, distributed & archived through Canisius College.
