Corporate Programs

The media coaching program is designed to help, guide, and mentor young students and professionals with aspirations of a career in media, or at the beginning of their careers.

Keynote Speaker

Kevin Sylvester and Paul Peck are able to draw on their vast experience and skills from their rolls in media and business to deliver a keynote speech that will leave the audience wanting more. Success, perseverance, and adapting to change are particular areas of expertise. Kevin and Paul prepare for speeches with the same voracity they do to broadcast a sporting event, with the audience in mind.

Emcee

Give your next corporate event, sales meeting, or fundraiser the professional touch with an emcee appearance from Kevin or Paul. They will engage your audience, and keep the pace of the event on track.

Media Coaching

Interviewed by the media on occasion? Are employees allowed to speak to the media? How do you use social media? We will provide strategies on what to expect when being interviewed, the do’s and don’ts of being interviewed, and things to consider with social media.
