ADDY Award Winning CMYK Publication

2016

Design concept proposal for a literary magazine based on geometric shapes and a pure CMYK color scheme. Design received a Silver ADDY through the regional level American Advertising Awards from AAF Buffalo.

Trying to understand the vision for this year’s Quadrangle, I determined the theme to be “organized chaos” with an overall look that suggest creativity. We were advised to use handlettered/ decorative typeefaces and colors that were bring but not neon.

I found a very fun and decorative font for the cover, this font becomes a graphic element in itself. Within the book, I used a thinner, handwriting font with a blocky, bold font to create contrast in the headlines. I set the actual stories in Avenir Next. Since these are the art on their own, I believe that they didn’t need any distracting embellishment.
