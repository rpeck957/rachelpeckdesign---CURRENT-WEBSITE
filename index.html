Welcome to my portfolio!

In college, I set a goal to hand code my own portfolio website. Having never written a line of code before, I had an enormous project ahead of me. So many wonderful tools exist that can bypass this, but I wanted to be able to create something completely on my own & learn along the way.

This website is an evolution of a lot of ideas, late nights, endless cups of coffee, frustration & curious persistence. It represents who I am as a person, my aesthetic as a designer & displays the work that I am most proud of in my career thus far. | Learn more about me!

Thank you for visiting, I am so proud of this website and I so appreciate that you stopped by! I would love to connect & hear your thoughts on my work, discuss working together or just chat. | Send me a message!
