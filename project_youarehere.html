You Are Here Starbucks Commercial

2015

During my internship at The Martin Group, my major project during the semester was to create and design an onboarding kit for interns. The design was executed according to the new agency brand and new brand elements were implemented to create a cohesive collateral piece within the brand.

This piece was created with the intention to be easily placed into a binder or folder with additional materials necessary for an intern. The title pages serve as dividers for each section and can contain a variety of collateral materials.
